Biography

I am an interdisciplinary independent researcher working at the intersection of technology innovation, consumer behavior, and sustainable product ecosystems. My research explores how emerging technologies—particularly artificial intelligence, machine learning, digital platforms, and smart systems—can transform consumer experiences, business models, and product development across industries.

My work spans multiple applied domains including retail innovation, fintech systems, digital marketing, customer engagement platforms, and smart device ecosystems. A significant portion of my research investigates the use of computational approaches such as machine learning, computer vision, and data-driven systems for improving decision making, personalization, and service design.

Alongside digital technologies, I conduct research on consumer product innovation, including food science, cosmetic formulations, packaging systems, and sustainable materials. Many of these projects examine circular economy approaches, biodegradable materials, and environmentally responsible alternatives in consumer goods and packaging.

Another major focus of my work is strategic marketing, branding, and socio-cultural consumption patterns, particularly in emerging digital environments such as social media platforms, online marketplaces, and immersive retail ecosystems. My research also explores how behavioral insights and design thinking can inform new products, services, and customer experiences.

Through an interdisciplinary approach that combines technology, market strategy, material science, and consumer research, my work aims to generate forward-looking insights into the evolution of future industries, digital economies, and sustainable innovation systems.
